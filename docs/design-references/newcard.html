What makes a good card
Front: 30 words max. One question testing one atomic piece of knowledge. If the answer needs two ideas, make two cards.
Back: 60 words max. A direct answer with just enough context. Not a paragraph. The constraint is the point.
Use formatting sparingly. Bold a key term. Code-wrap a function name. The card should scan in under 5 seconds.
Cloze cards: use _____ on the front, answer on the back.
